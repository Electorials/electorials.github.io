Electorials was established on March 2nd, 2017 with a goal to provide an easy, convenient website for the electronics community to share.
Our site is non-profit, relying completely on free web hosting and components sponsored for reviews. Feel free to share our projects, tutorials & reviews and we welcome any sponsors, comments or suggestions to improve the site's content.
You can contact us anytime and we will endeavour to reply back as soon as possible.

A Guide to our Projects

Our projects offer a wide variety of content for the entire electornics community from projects suitable for beginners all the way to advanced makers. In each project, photos of the circuit functioning and all the necessary components are included. Each project is set up in this format:

  1. Introduction - a brief explanation of the project (components, function, etc.)
  2. Circuit diagram and schematics
  3. Project code
  4. Explanation of the code

Additional content may be included depending on the type of project.
